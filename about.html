Who We Are

Engineering & art,
together.

We're a couple of engineers and artists — one a painter, both tinkerers — who spent years designing, 3D printing, and building things from the ground up just for the joy of it.

Between us we bring together mechanical engineering, fine art painting, 3D fabrication, and client-facing work. We know how to design something beautiful and how to build it so it actually works — and lasts.

"Your window is your loudest voice on the street — and most shops are whispering."
— Vitrine Taj
The Moment It Started

Travelling Europe,
something kept stopping us.

From Paris to Florence, Amsterdam to Lisbon — everywhere we went, small independent boutiques had these incredible, living window displays that made you feel something before you even stepped inside.

Wonder. Curiosity. That irresistible pull to get closer. Sometimes you'd stand there for several minutes just to figure out how it worked. The craftsmanship. The theatre. The story being told without a single word.

We came home and couldn't stop thinking: why don't shops here feel like that? The craft, the storytelling, the artistry of a beautiful window — it seemed like a lost art, available only to big brands with enormous budgets.

So we decided to change that. Vitrine Taj was born to bring that magic back — one window at a time.
