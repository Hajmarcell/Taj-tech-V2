What We Create

Every display
built from nothing.

No templates. No catalogue parts. No off-the-shelf props. Every Vitrine Taj display is custom designed and custom built — from the first sketch to the final screw.

⚙️
001
Kinetic & Automated Displays
Custom-engineered motion mechanisms — rotating, oscillating, rising and falling — that bring your display to life around the clock. We design and build everything in-house, from the motor logic to the final finish.
→ Motion Design · Automation · Electronics
🖨️
002
3D Printed Sculpture & Props
One-of-a-kind sculptural pieces fabricated specifically for your brand — from intricate geometric forms to figurative characters. Nothing off-the-shelf. Every piece designed to tell your story.
→ Custom Fabrication · Sculpture · 3D Design
🎨
003
Painted Art & Illustration
Hand-painted backdrops, illustrated scenes, and original artwork that give your display depth and atmosphere. A painterly touch that no screen or print can replicate.
→ Fine Art · Hand-Painted · Illustration
✨
004
Seasonal & Themed Displays
Holiday windows, launch campaigns, seasonal refreshes — complete themed experiences that keep your storefront feeling current and alive all year round.
→ Seasonal · Campaign · Refresh
📐
005
Concept & Brand Storytelling
Before anything is built, we develop the narrative. What's the mood? The palette? The one thing a passerby should feel? We art-direct the full concept so every element works together.
→ Art Direction · Concept · Brand
🔧
006
Full Install & Setup
We handle delivery, installation, and testing ourselves. Your display arrives turnkey — everything secured, motion tested, and ready to run. Maintenance and seasonal swap-outs available.
→ Installation · Maintenance · Support
