How It Works

From first
conversation to
opening night.

01
First contact
Tell us about your vision.

Fill out the brief form or drop us an email. Tell us about your shop, your customers, and what you dream your window could feel like. No idea is too vague — that's exactly where we like to start.

02
Discovery
We get to know your shop.

We visit in person (or via a video call) to understand your space, your brand, your customers, and your street. Every window is different — the light, the foot traffic, the season. We design for your specific context.

03
Concept
A display concept built for you.

We develop a full concept — mood, narrative, materials, motion — and present it to you with sketches and mood boards. You approve everything before we build a single component. This is your window.

04
Build
Designed, printed, painted, built.

Our workshop produces everything in-house — 3D printed forms, hand-painted surfaces, engineered motion mechanisms. Every piece is made to last and made to impress.

05
Install
We install it ourselves.

No crates, no instructions, no DIY. We show up, we install everything, we test it, and we walk you through how it all works. Your display goes live turnkey.

06
After
Ongoing support & seasonal updates.

We're available after opening night — for maintenance, seasonal refreshes, or a completely new concept. Many of our clients update their windows every season.

Ready to start the conversation?

The first step is just a short form. Tell us about your shop and your vision — we'll take it from there.
