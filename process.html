The Process

Four steps. You're involved in every one.

01
Week 1
Discovery Call

We start with a free, no-pressure conversation. Tell us about your shop — your brand, your customers, what you sell, what you want people to feel. No formal brief needed. We ask the right questions.

By the end of the call, we'll have a clear sense of what your window could become — and you'll have a sense of whether we're the right fit.

Free · No commitment · 45–60 minutes · Video or phone
02
Weeks 2–3
Concept & Design

We develop a full concept — mood boards, colour palette, structural sketches, and where applicable, 3D previews of the final display. You review and approve everything before a single component is fabricated.

This is where the magic starts to feel real. You'll see your window taking shape for the first time.

Mood boards · Sketches · 3D previews · Revision rounds included
03
Weeks 4–7
Build & Fabricate

We print, paint, engineer, wire, and assemble every element in our studio. Progress photos and updates throughout — you're never left wondering what's happening.

Every kinetic component is tested extensively before we pack it up. Nothing leaves the studio until it works perfectly.

In-studio fabrication · Progress updates · Full QA before shipping
04
Final Week
Install & Launch

We come to you. We set everything up, test every motion sequence, adjust for your specific window dimensions, and make sure it looks exactly right. We don't leave until it's perfect.

After install, you get a full handover — how everything works, how to turn it on and off, and how to reach us if anything needs attention.

On-site install · Full handover · Ongoing support available
Common Questions

Things people usually ask us.

How long does a project take?
Most projects run 6–8 weeks from discovery call to install. Simpler displays can be faster; more complex kinetic builds may take a little longer. We'll give you a clear timeline after our first conversation.
Do I need to know what I want before reaching out?
Not at all. Many clients come to us with nothing more than "I want my window to feel magical." That's enough to start. We're good at drawing ideas out through conversation — that's what the discovery call is for.
What does it cost?
Every project is custom, so pricing varies. We'll discuss budget openly in our first call — there's no awkwardness about it. We work with a range of budgets and will always be honest about what's achievable within yours.
Do you do seasonal swaps?
Yes. Many clients work with us on an ongoing basis — updating their display for the holidays, new seasons, or product launches. We offer maintenance and swap-out packages.
Do you travel for installs?
Yes. We travel to install every display ourselves — we won't ship you a crate and wish you luck. Travel costs are factored into the project quote up front, so there are no surprises.
