The Process

Four steps. You're
involved in every one.

01
Week 1
Discovery Call

We start with a free, no-pressure conversation. Tell us about your shop — your brand, your customers, what you sell, what you want people to feel. No formal brief needed.

By the end of the call, we'll have a clear sense of what your window could become — and you'll have a sense of whether we're the right fit.

Free · No commitment · 45–60 minutes · Video or phone
02
Weeks 2–3
Concept & Design

We develop a full concept — mood boards, colour palette, structural sketches, and where applicable, 3D previews of the final display. You review and approve everything before a single component is fabricated.

This is where the magic starts to feel real.

Mood boards · Sketches · 3D previews · Revision rounds included
03
Weeks 4–7
Build & Fabricate

We print, paint, engineer, wire, and assemble every element in our studio. Progress photos and updates throughout — you're never left wondering what's happening.

Every kinetic component is tested extensively before we pack it up. Nothing leaves until it works perfectly.

In-studio fabrication · Progress updates · Full QA before delivery
04
Final Week
Install & Launch

We come to you. We set everything up, test every motion sequence, and make sure it looks exactly right in your actual window. We don't leave until it's perfect.

Full handover on how everything works, plus ongoing support.

On-site install · Full handover · Ongoing support available
Common Questions

Things people
usually ask us.

How long does a project take?
Most projects run 6–8 weeks from discovery call to install. Simpler displays can be faster; more complex kinetic builds may take longer. We'll give you a clear timeline after our first call.
Do I need to know what I want before reaching out?
Not at all. Many clients come to us with nothing more than "I want my window to feel magical." That's enough. We're good at drawing ideas out through conversation.
What does it cost?
Every project is custom, so pricing varies. We discuss budget openly in our first call — no awkwardness. We work with a range of budgets and will always be honest about what's achievable.
Do you do seasonal swaps?
Yes. Many clients work with us ongoing — updating their display for holidays, new seasons, or launches. We offer maintenance and swap-out packages.
Do you travel for installs?
Yes. We travel to every install ourselves — we won't ship you a crate and wish you luck. Travel costs are factored into the project quote up front.
